\[
\begin{array}{r@{\;=\big\{(}r@{,}r@{),(}r@{,}r@{),(}r@{,}r@{),(}r@{,}r@{),(}r@{,}r@{)\big\}}}

g&-7&-4&1&-8&5&1&7&0&9&-7\\
h&-7&0&-1&-5&1&-7&7&-1&9&-5
\end{array}
\]

\mitemxx

{$\Gp{g+f}(x)=x^3+10x^2-12x-24$}
\mitemxx

\mitemxx
{$\Gp{h+k}(x)=3x^2-18x-12$}

Write expressions for each of the following. Do not simplify your answer.
\begin{smenum}
\mitemxx
{$\Gp{\dfrac{f}{h}}(x)=\dfrac{3x^2-8x+4}{x-2}$}
{$\Gp{\dfrac{g}{h}}(x)=\dfrac{x^3+7x^2-4x-28}{x-2}$}
