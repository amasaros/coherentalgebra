\subsection{Handling Leading Coefficients} 
Leading coefficients are easy to deal with, since the \makeblank[.5in] of a number doesn't vary.
\begin{Example}
Solve the polynomial inequality
\[
-3(x+4)^2(x-7)(x-2) < 0
\]
\begin{cpic}{}{10}
\foreach \x/\lbl in {-3/{$-3\ \ $},-4/{$(x+4)^2$},-5/{$x-7\ \ $},-6/{$x-2\ \ $},-8/Product}
	\regtextright{-6}{\x}{\lbl};
\regtextleftlp{1}{-10}{Solution set:};
\draw[style=axis] (-6,-1) -- (6,-1);
\foreach \x in {-3,0,3} \draw[style=tick] (\x,-.75) -- (\x,-1.25);
\end{cpic}
\end{Example}
