\section{Polynomial Division}
In this section, we will study the last operation on polynomials: polynomial division. Like division of whole numbers, division of polynomials presents some complications, but is useful in many ways. In particular, we will use division in order to identify factors of polynomials when they cannot be guessed easily using our basic techniques.

We begin with a review of the long division of whole numbers before meeting the basic algorithm for polynomial division. We then discuss the various difficulties that arise in polynomial division that do not come up in long division of numbers. Finally, we see an example which brings together the entire process.

An alternate method, more popular outside the US, is also discussed. Some students find this method easier. You may use whichever method you prefer.
