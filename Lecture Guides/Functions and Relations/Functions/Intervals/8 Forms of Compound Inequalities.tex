\subsection{8 Forms of Compound Inequalities} 
When we solve a compound inequality, one of these eight things will happen.
\begin{icpic}
\useasboundingbox (0,1) rectangle (0.5\textwidth,-0.9\textheight);
\foreach \x/\y/\lbl in {0/0/{$x\leq 8$ and $x<-3$}, %and - both left
												1/0/{$x<-2$ or $x\leq 3$}, %or - both left
												0/1/{$x\geq 9$ and $x\geq 5$}, %and - both right
												1/1/{$x\geq -3$ or $x> 7$}, %or - both right
												0/2/{$x<4$ and $x\geq -1$}, %and - overlap
												1/2/{$x\geq -5$ or $x\leq 7$}, %or - overlap
												0/3/{$x>-2$ and $x<-4$}, %and - gap
												1/3/{$x>9$ or $x\leq -8$}  %or - gap
											}
{
	\begin{scope}[xshift=\x*0.5\textwidth, yshift=-\y*0.225\textheight]
		\draw (0,0) node[anchor=base]{\lbl};
		\draw[axis] (-4,-1.5) -- (4,-1.5);
		\foreach \z in {-2,2} \draw[tick] (\z,-1.5+0.2) -- (\z,-1.5-0.2);
		\foreach \z/\lbz in {-3.5/Single Inequality,-4.5/Set-Builder Not'n,-5.5/Interval Notation}
		{
			\regtextright{0}{\z}{\lbz:};
			\draw[npblue] (0,\z) -- (1.5in,\z);
		}
	\end{scope}
}
