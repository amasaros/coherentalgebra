\section{Relations and Functions}
The only other idea in the theory of mathematics as fundemental as the idea of a set is the idea of a function. In this section, we will define a ``relation'' in terms of sets, and a function as a special kind of relation. In the next section, we will see another notion of functions, and how the two are connected.
