\subsection{Polynomials} 
\begin{definition}[Polynomial]
A \term{polynomial} (or \term{polynomial expression}) is an expression given as a sum of one or more monomials. The monomials are called the \term{terms} of the polynomial.
\end{definition}
\begin{Example}
For each expression below, determine whether or not it is a polynomial. If it is not, explain why not.
\begin{enumerate}\setabc
\item $3x^2+5x-7$\makeblanknewf
\item $x-3\sqrt{x}+2$\makeblanknewf
\item $4x^{-2}-3$\makeblanknewf
\item $2x^3y$\makeblanknewf
\item $5$\makeblanknewf
\end{enumerate}
\end{Example}
