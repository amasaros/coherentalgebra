\subsection{Multiple Iterations} 
We must continue factoring until all remaining factors are prime.
\begin{example}
Fully factor $5x^6+15x^5-5x^2-15x$.
\begin{lpic}{}{13}
\foreach \x/\step in {-1/1. Factor out the GCF,
											-3/2. Factor the polynomial factor,
											-7/3. Factor each new polynomial factor,
											-12/4. Put it all together}
	\regtextleft{0}{\x}{\step};
\end{lpic}
\end{example}
