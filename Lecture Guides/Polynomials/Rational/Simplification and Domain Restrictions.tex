\subsection{Simplification and Domain Restrictions} 
When we simplify the expression for a rational function, \emph{the domain stays the same}. Sometimes, we must note a domain restriction.
\begin{example}
Let $h(x)=\dfrac{3x^2-2x-8}{x^2+4x-12}$.
