\subsection{Roots Within Roots} 
If we are taking a root of a root, we will use the properties of rational exponents to rewrite.
\begin{Example}
Rewrite each expression as a single simplified radical. Assume all variables represent positive real numbers.
\begin{lpic}{}{9}
\foreach \y/\exp in {1/{\sqrt[3]{\sqrt{x}}},3/{\sqrt{x\sqrt[5]{x^2}}},5/{\sqrt[3]{2x\sqrt{3xy}}}}
\regtextleft{0.25}{-\y}{$\exp={}$};
\end{lpic}
\end{Example}
