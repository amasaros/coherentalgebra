\subsection{The Quadratic Formula} 
Completing the square will always get us the solutions to a quadratic equation (if they exist), but it's tedious. We can complete the square ``in general'' and get a formula for the solutions.

\begin{fact}[The Quadratic Formula]
For a quadratic equation $ax^2+bx+c=0$, the solutions (if any) are given by the formula
$$x=\dfrac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$$
\end{fact}
