\section{Graphs of Polynomials}
In this section, we will discuss the basic properties of the graphs of polynomials. We will not be able to fully graph polynomials---determining the exact coordinates of a point requires either more advanced techniques, or more involved algebra than we want to be involved with---but we will be able to recognize the shape of a graph of a polynomial, and an expression for a polynomial function from its graph.
