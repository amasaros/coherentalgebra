\subsection{A Polynomial Inequality in Standard Form} 
The standard form of a polynomial equation is
\[
P(x)=0
\]
Similarly, the standard form of a polynomial inequality is one of
\[
P(x)<0
\quad
P(x)>0
\quad
P(x)\leq 0
\quad
P(x)\geq 0
\]
Given a polynomial inequality in standard form, we must \makeblank[1.5in] the polynomial first.
\begin{example}
Solve
\[
-2x^3-4x^2+18x-4\geq 0
\]
\fillpaper
\end{example}
