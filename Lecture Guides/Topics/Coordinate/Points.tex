\subsection{Points in the Plane} 
In geometry, a \emph{point} is one of the key undefined concepts.
\begin{itemize}
\item It has \makeblank[1in] but no \makeblank[1in]. 
\item We denote points with \makeblank[1in] letters.
\item In the plane, we will refer to a point by its \makeblank[1.5in].
\end{itemize}
