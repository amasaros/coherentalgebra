\subsection{Step Functions} 
A ``step function'' is a piecewise function whose pieces are all constant.
\begin{example}
Sketch the graph of $f(x)=\pw{-2&-3&\leq&x&<&-1\\3&-1&\leq&x&\leq&1\\1&1&<&x&\leq&3}$

\end{lpic}
\end{example}
