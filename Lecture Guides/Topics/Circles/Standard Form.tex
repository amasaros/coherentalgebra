\subsection{Circles in Standard Form} 
\begin{displaybox}[Equation of a Circle (standard form)]
An equation of a circle in \emph{standard} form is of the form
\[
Ax^2+Ay^2+Cx+Dy+F=0
\]
with $A$, $C$, $D$, and $F$ integers, $A>0$
\end{displaybox}
